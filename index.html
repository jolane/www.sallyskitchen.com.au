About Sally

Sally fell passionately in love with food in her mother’s cozy kitchen. Now she has her own kitchen – a vibrant and friendly place to enjoy delicious, traditional recipes and good company around the table.

Sally’s Kitchen serves deliciously simple,
modern food to be enjoyed with fine
wine and great company.

Sally’s food is fresh, flavourful and made with love. Using seasonal produce, her eclectic menu features anything and everything that takes her fancy. Simple dishes are created with care, integrity and quality ingredients. This is feel good food, just like Sally’s mother made.



THE KITCHEN

You’ll find Sally in the kitchen or chatting to guests most days. Take the time to sit and stay for a chat and sample what’s cooking. It’s that type of local – where everyone loves their job and knows just how you like your coffee.
